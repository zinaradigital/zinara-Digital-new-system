What CRM Logic Solves

Failure Point: Leads arrive but manual entry fails, data is inconsistent, and sales stages are guessed rather than tracked.

Solution: Automated lead capture with intelligent routing based on source, intent, and customer profile. Every interaction updates the CRM automatically.

Key Features

  • Automatic Lead Assignment: Leads route to correct salesperson based on territory, product, or priority.
  • Stage Progression Rules: Deals move through pipeline stages based on defined triggers (demo completed, quote sent, etc.).
  • Deal Probability Scoring: AI calculates win probability based on engagement, timeline, and historical patterns.
  • Pipeline Health Monitoring: Real-time alerts when deals stall or velocity drops.

What Workflow Automation Solves

Failure Point: Follow-ups are manual, inconsistent, or forgotten. Response time varies wildly. No systematic recovery.

Solution: Conditional workflows that trigger based on time, behavior, and context. Every lead gets perfect follow-up without human intervention.

Workflow Types

  • Lead Nurturing Sequences: Multi-touch campaigns across email, WhatsApp, SMS based on engagement.
  • Recovery Workflows: Automatic re-engagement when leads go cold or deals stall.
  • Customer Onboarding: Sequential task automation for new customer setup.
  • Event-Based Triggers: Workflows activate on specific actions (form submit, email open, meeting scheduled).

Example Workflow: Lead Recovery

Day 0: Lead enters system via form
Day 0 (immediate): Auto-reply email sent
Day 1: WhatsApp message if no response
Day 3: Email with case study
Day 7: Final outreach with special offer
If still no response: Move to long-term nurture campaign

What Communication Routing Solves

Failure Point: Messages arrive via WhatsApp, email, forms—but they're fragmented, delayed, or lost. No unified tracking.

Solution: All channels route to single CRM pipeline with automatic classification, prioritization, and assignment.

Channel Integrations

  • WhatsApp Business API: Two-way messaging, template automation, quick replies.
  • Email Parsing: Inbound emails auto-create leads, extract intent, attach to existing records.
  • Web Forms: Submissions route to correct pipeline based on form type and content.
  • SMS Gateway: Bulk messaging and transactional notifications.

Routing Intelligence

Messages classified by intent (sales inquiry, support request, billing issue) using keyword detection and AI. Priority assigned based on customer value, urgency, and context.

What Revenue Dashboards Solve

Failure Point: You know marketing spend and sales closed—but can't connect them. Attribution is guessed. ROI is unknown.

Solution: Real-time dashboards showing exactly which campaigns generate revenue, where leads stall, and what actions drive conversion.

Dashboard Metrics

  • Revenue Attribution: Which marketing source/campaign/channel produced each sale.
  • Pipeline Velocity: Average time from lead to close, by source and stage.
  • Conversion Funnels: Drop-off rates at each stage, diagnostic alerts.
  • Revenue Forecasting: Projected monthly/quarterly revenue based on current pipeline.
  • Team Performance: Individual and team-level activity and results.

Access Levels

Executive View: High-level revenue, forecasts, ROI
Manager View: Team performance, pipeline health, conversion rates
Rep View: Personal tasks, follow-ups, deal status
